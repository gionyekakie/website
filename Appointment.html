How to Book an Appointment

  1. Select the service you desire from this page.
  2. Write the specifications and include add on's if any.
  3. Choose a date and time that fits your schedule.
  4. Wait for it to process and check if the time is available.
  5. Provide the required info requested.
  6. Pay a 20% deposit to secure your appointment.
  7. Arrive on time with your confirmation email and enjoy your hair transformation!!
